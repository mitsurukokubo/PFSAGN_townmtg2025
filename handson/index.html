Procedures for participating in a hands-on session

To access to the PFS Science Platform (SP, where PFS test data and jupyter notebooks are available), please complete the following steps by February 10, 5:00 PM (JST) :
  • Make sure you are eligible to hold membership of PFS Collaboration (ref. https://pfs.ipmu.jp/research/index.html)
  • Register for the PFS Collaboration: Visit the registration form at https://pfs.ipmu.jp/research/regist_collab.html
  • Email the LOC Representatives: after registering for the PFS Collaboration, send an email to the LOC representatives (Ichikawa & Kokubo)*. Include your name, affiliation, and status (e.g., faculty, postdoc, PhD/Master student). The LOC representatives send back the link to the application form for the SP access
  • Apply for SP Access: after receiving the SP access link from the LOC representatives, complete the SP access application.

Ichikawa: ichikawa.waseda ATM gmail.com
Kokubo: mitsuru.kokubo ATM nao.ac.jp
