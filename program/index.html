PFS hands-on session

We will hold a hands-on session on the afternoon of the second day, where participants will work with genuine PFS data collected during the PFS engineering runs.
Only those who meet the following requirements can join the session:
(1) Membership in the PFS Collaboration
(2) Registration for SP access (registration for this month closed on February 10)
