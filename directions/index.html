Temporary Closure of NAOJ Mitaka Campus Main Gate and Entry via Rear Gate

From January 8 until March 30, 2025 (Scheduled), the Main Gate of NAOJ Mitaka Campus is closed due to construction work in the campus. During this time, we ask all guests to use the Rear Gate. For further information, please refer to the website below.　
(https://www.nao.ac.jp/en/notice/20241227-entrance.html)

Access

A. Access to NAOJ Mitaka campus by public transportation
B. Entry via Rear Gate (near the "Tenmondai Ura" bus stop)
C. Stop by the Temporary Guard House to receive a temporary access card (IC card)
 * Visitors who need to enter the Subaru building must borrow the IC card at the Guard House.
 * People listed on the participant list of this workshop (except those from NAOJ, IoA, and SOKENDAI) have been pre-registered as visitors at the Guard House. By stating that you are attending the "2nd PFS-AGN Town Meeting" and presenting an ID card (such as a student ID or passport), you will receive a temporary access card.
 * If the visit is scheduled for several days, the visitor is not required to return the card each day. Do not forget to return the card to the Guard House on the last day of your visit.
D. Head to the Subaru Building 1st Floor, Large seminar room (NAOJ Conference Room Map)
